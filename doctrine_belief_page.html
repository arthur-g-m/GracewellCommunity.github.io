Doctrine Belief

Our Statement of Faith

At Gracewell Methodist Church, our beliefs are rooted in the timeless teachings of the Christian faith. We hold to a firm and unshakable foundation of biblical truth, and our statement of faith serves as a guiding light for our congregation. We believe in the Triune God - Father, Son, and Holy Spirit - who is the source of all creation, redemption, and transformation. We affirm the authority and inspiration of the Holy Scriptures as the infallible Word of God, serving as our ultimate guide in matters of faith and practice.

Salvation by Grace

Central to our doctrine is the belief in salvation by God's grace alone. We firmly hold that salvation is a gift freely given to all who put their trust in Jesus Christ as their Lord and Savior. It is not through our own efforts or good works that we are saved, but solely through the redemptive work of Jesus on the cross. We celebrate the forgiveness of sins and the promise of eternal life for all who confess their faith in Him. Our church is a place where individuals from all walks of life can experience the life-transforming power of God's grace.

The Priesthood of All Believers

We embrace the doctrine of the priesthood of all believers, recognizing that every member of our congregation is a valuable and essential part of our church family. Each person has unique gifts, talents, and a vital role to play in the body of Christ. We encourage active participation in ministry and service, empowering our members to use their God-given abilities to make a positive impact in our community and the world. Together, we strive to fulfill the Great Commission by spreading the love of Christ to all nations.

Christian Unity and Love

At Gracewell Methodist Church, we emphasize the importance of Christian unity and love. We believe that, as followers of Christ, we are called to love one another as He loved us. Our doors are open to all who seek to know and serve the Lord, regardless of background, race, or denomination. We strive to foster a welcoming and inclusive community where individuals can grow in faith, share their journey, and support one another in times of joy and adversity. Our belief in the power of Christ's love compels us to reach out to the hurting, the marginalized, and the lost, extending His grace to all.
