Appointments

Instructions

Welcome to the GraceWell Methodist appointment system, here we pride ourselves and are strong believers in staying in touch and aware of every members wellbeing in our community, as such please know that we are delighted and encourage to make use of our appointment scheduling system. Whether you're seeking professional advice, medical assistance, or just want to talk, we're here for you, here to help. Our Community will be more that dedicated to providing you with exceptional care and support.

To book an appointment, please follow the steps below:

  1. Select the service you require from the list provided.
  2. Choose a preferred date and time slot that suits your schedule.
  3. Fill in your contact details, including your name, phone number, and email address.
  4. Provide any specific information or additional notes that might be relevant to your appointment.
