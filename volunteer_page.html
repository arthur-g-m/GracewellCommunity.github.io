Events for Volunteering


Feed the Needy

Come feed the needy and be a source of hope the hungry. Your time and compassion can nourish both body and soul. Join us in this fulfilling mission to bring warmth, nourishment, and smiles to those in need.

Youth Service

Elevate the faith of the youth by becoming a youth leader! Our Youth Ministry is a dynamic space where faith, friendships, and personal development converge. Join us for exciting events, engaging discussions, and opportunities to make a positive impact in our community.

Special Preaching

Join us for a special preaching service that promises inspiration and spiritual enrichment. Let's come together and explore the timeless wisdom of the Word. See you there!


Instructions

1. Provide Your Personal Information: Begin by entering your First Name and Last Name. Additionally, please share your Cellphone number for contact purposes, and feel free to include your Telephone number if you wish. We'll need your Email Address to reach out to you regarding your volunteer registration, so please make sure it's accurate. Enter your Date of Birth to help us better coordinate volunteer opportunities.

2. Select the Volunteer Event: Choose the church-related activity or event you're interested in volunteering for from the dropdown list under Volunteer Event. Options may include Ushers, Feed the Needy, Youth Service, Media Team, and more.

3. Confirmation Email: After submitting the form, you will promptly receive a confirmation email confirming your successful volunteer registration. Our church team will review your registration and contact you through the email address you provided. We'll discuss further details and next steps for your volunteer involvement.
